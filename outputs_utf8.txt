2.  🎯  Problem  Statement  
Small  businesses  struggle  with:  
●  Managing  multiple  types  of  financial  obligations  (rent,  salaries,  vendors,  taxes,  etc.)  ●  Deciding  payment  priorities  under  limited  cash  flow  ●  Understanding  trade-offs  between  penalties,  relationships,  and  survival  ●  Communicating  with  vendors  during  delays  ●  Utilizing  surplus  capital  effectively  
Existing  tools:  
●  Track  finances  ❌  ●  Automate  bookkeeping  ❌  ●  But  do  not  make  decisions  ❌  
 
3.  💡  Solution  
Full  Stack  CFO  introduces  a  decision-first  financial  system  that:  
●  Aggregates  all  financial  obligations  ●  Uses  deterministic  logic  to  prioritize  payments  ●  Generates  multiple  payment  strategies  ●  Explains  decisions  in  simple  language  ●  Automates  vendor  communication  
 
●  Suggests  business  opportunities  when  surplus  exists  
 
4.  🧠  Core  Principles  
4.1  Deterministic  +  AI  Hybrid  Architecture  
●  Python  Engine  →  All  financial  calculations  and  decisions  ●  LLM  (Gemini  API)  →  Language,  explanation,  and  reasoning  
AI  never  performs  arithmetic  —  ensuring  reliability  and  trust.  
 
4.2  Obligation-Centric  Model  
Instead  of  “bills,”  the  system  models  all  business  obligations ,  including:  
●  Fixed  costs  (salary,  rent,  tax,  EMI)  ●  Operational  costs  (utilities,  subscriptions)  ●  Vendor  payments  ●  Discretionary  spending  
 
4.3  Explainability  First  
Every  decision  is:  
●  Transparent  ●  Justified  ●  Human-readable
